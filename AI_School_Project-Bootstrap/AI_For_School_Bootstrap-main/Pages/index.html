Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

I have no prior knowledge of Artificial Intelligence. Can I solve the problems ?

In this project we are mainly focus on 3 types of problem : i) Constrain Satisfaction; ii) Automated Problem Solving; iii) Logical Deduction. Questions based on this topic are fundamental and basic implementation of Logical thinking. You don't need to worry about any prerequisite, you can easily do it by a little bit of try

Is there any backward free navigation so I can revisit my question that has been previously attempted ?

Yes, there will be backward free navigation so, you can reattempt your previously attempted question .

Does it help me in my school exam as well ?

This practice is just a brain muscle game. So, if you practice those question and learn them honestly they will definitely help you in the logical thinking subjects like Maths, Science. As, well as they can build your foundation of Artificial Intelligence.
